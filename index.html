Our Legacy

Established in 2018, LPA has become the most trusted physical training institute in Lakhisarai district, producing successful candidates for Army, Police, SSC GD, and Central Armed Police Forces.

Prime Location

Located near the historic K.R.K Ground, Lakhisarai, our training ground offers authentic open-field experience perfect for defence preparation.

Our Mission

To empower youth of rural Bihar with world-class physical training and make them ready for defence services, building a strong, disciplined generation.
